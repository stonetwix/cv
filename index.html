About me

I'm currently studying to become a front end developer. The decision to learn JavaScript is one of the best I ever made. My interest in web development began eight years ago when I took a course in web design, since then I have created many websites for customers and companies, both in Sweden and internationally. I have worked as a graphic designer for about 14 years, which I benefit greatly from in my role as a web developer. I am also previously trained in UX and UI design and think that accessibility and the user perspective are important aspects in everything I create. I also have an education in journalism. In a professional role, I’m creative, efficient and independent. I hope we get in touch!
